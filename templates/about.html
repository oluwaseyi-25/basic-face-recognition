About Attendance Tracker

Attendance Tracker is a modern, biometric-based attendance management system designed to streamline and secure the process of tracking attendance in academic and corporate environments. By leveraging facial recognition technology and IoT integration, the system ensures accuracy, efficiency, and security.

Features

  • Real-time face recognition for attendance logging.
  • Integration with IoT devices for decentralized tracking.
  • Secure storage of attendance records and user data.
  • User-friendly web interface for administrators and users.
  • Comprehensive reporting and data export capabilities.
